ARTIFY UI

EVENT RULES

  1. The event will consist of two rounds:
    • Round 1: Rapid Sketch – Participants will be given a problem statement and must create a wireframe/mockup in a limited time. Judges will shortlist entries based on creativity and clarity.
    • Round 2: UI Build Arena – Qualified participants will design a full-fledged UI using tools like Figma, Adobe XD, or any design software. Designs will be judged on aesthetics, usability, and innovation.
  2. Allowed Tools:
    • Round 1: Paper sketches, Figma, or Adobe XD (wireframe).
    • Round 2: Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, or similar design platforms.
  3. Evaluation Criteria:
    • Creativity & Originality
    • Relevance to the given problem statement
  4. General Rules:
    • Participants compete individually (no teams).
    • Internet access is allowed, but plagiarism or use of pre-made templates is strictly prohibited.
    • Judges’ decisions will be final and binding.
