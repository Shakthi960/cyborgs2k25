WELCOME TO THE DIGITAL REVOLUTION

"CYBORGS 2K25, on October 25, 2025, is a national-level technical symposium where innovation meets execution. At SRM Valliammai Engineering College, coders, hackers, and tech visionaries converge to push boundaries in AI, cybersecurity, coding, and emerging tech. Through competitive challenges, collaborative hackathons, and cutting-edge workshops, participants will decode complex problems and engineer futuristic solutions. This is more than an event—it's a launchpad for the next generation of technologists. Ready to build, break, and redefine the future?"

EVENTSEVENTS

PAPER NOVA

Step into the spotlight of innovation with Paper Nova, where budding minds showcase computer science brilliance. From crisp presentations to engaging Q&A sessions, this platform celebrates research, creativity, and fresh ideas that shape tomorrow’s digital frontier.

CODE CARNIVAL

Dive into the electrifying world of Code Carnival, where coding meets cryptography. With thrilling rounds on programming, databases, and logic puzzles, this battle of brains crowns the sharpest coders ready to conquer tomorrow’s toughest technological challenges.

ARTIFY UI

Unleash your creativity at Artify UI, where imagination meets innovation. Craft stunning interfaces with code or design tools, turning simple problem statements into engaging digital experiences. Build, design, and impress with user-friendly solutions that captivate every judge.

RECODEX

Enter the thrilling challenge of Recodex, where logic rules and algorithms dominate. Decode puzzles, match outputs with smart solutions, and craft codes that surprise judges. Showcase your ingenuity by solving problems with unique approaches that redefine coding brilliance.

STRANGER'S BATTLE

Gear up for Strange Battle, the ultimate coding duel where speed, skill, and strategy matter. From SQL queries to logic puzzles, face off in intense one-on-one clashes that crown the fastest, fiercest problem solver as champion.

CACHE CHAOS

Embrace the unpredictable thrill of Cache Chaos, a contest mixing wit with technical mastery. Solve riddles, crack picture connections, and climb the Byte Ladder. Only sharp minds, quick thinking, and fearless teamwork can survive this high-energy competition.

PYSTACK (WORKSHOP)

Embark on a learning journey with PyStack, the workshop that transforms coding beginners into Python pros. From core concepts to building dynamic Django web apps, gain practical skills, expert insights, and certificates that elevate your programming career.
