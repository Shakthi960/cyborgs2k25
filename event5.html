STRANGER'S BATTLE

EVENT RULES

  1. Papers should focus on topics related to computer science.
  2. Teams must submit soft copies of their papers ahead of the event for review.
  3. Hard copies should be handed over to the judges prior to the presentation.
  4. Presentations will be followed by a Q&A session with judges and audience.
  5. Each team can have a maximum of 3 participants.
  6. Presentation time limit is 10 minutes followed by 5 minutes for Q&A.
  7. Plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification.
  8. Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
