STRANGERS BATTLE

EVENT RULES

  1. Event Structure: The event will be conducted in two rounds:
    1. Round 1: Techno Box 1v1 Coding Battle – Participants will randomly pick numbers. Those who receive matching numbers will compete in a 1v1 coding challenge with same problems. The first participant to pass all test cases will qualify for Round 2.
    2. Round 2: Vision Quest – The Memory & Mind ChallengeTest your observation and focus in this exciting visual memory round! Participants will see five images, each shown for just 5 seconds, and must pick the correct one from three options. Scores are based on accuracy and speed — the sharper your memory, the higher you rank!
    1. The first to submit with all test cases passed qualifies for Round 2, while the second participant’s selection will be decided by the judges if he/she also submit the Challenge.
  3. Allowed Languages:
    1. Round 1: Python, Java, SQL
  4. Evaluation Criteria:
    1. Round 1: Fastest participant to pass all test cases.
    2. Round 2: Speed and high score.
  5. General Rules:
    1. Each participant must compete individually (no teams allowed).
    2. The judges’ decisions will be final and binding.
