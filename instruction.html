How to Reach SRM Valliammai Engineering College

  • By Train
    Take any Chennai Beach – Tambaram – Chengalpattu suburban train and get down at Potheri Railway Station. The college is just a 12-minute walk from the station.
  • By Bus
    From different parts of Chennai, you can take MTC buses going towards Chengalpattu on GST Road. Get down at VPT / Valliammai College stop. Some bus numbers are 500, 500A, 500D, 500E, 500W, 118, 118ET. The campus is a short walk from the stop.
