EVENT RULES

  1. Total Rounds: 2
  2. Total Duration: Approximately 1 hour 45 minutes
  3. Round 1: BrainFreeze!
    1. Theme: "Team up, think fast, and qualify!"
    2. Duration: 60 minutes
    3. Flow:
      1. Team Selection: Teams are randomly assigned with unique names.
      2. Quick Topic Game (Icebreaker): Teams list items on a random topic (e.g., "Things we bring to college") within 30 seconds to earn points and set answering priority.
      3. Technical Question Rounds:
        1. True/False: 2 questions × 2 marks = 4 marks
        2. Picture Connection: 2 questions × 3 marks = 6 marks
        3. Pseudocode: 1 question × 5 marks = 5 marks
        4. Grand Total: 15 marks
      4. Evaluation: Top 6–8 teams qualify for Round 2.
  4. Round 2: Byte the Ladder — Race to 50
    1. Theme: "Snake & Ladder meets Brain & Logic!"
    2. Duration: 40–45 minutes
    3. Game Concept: A classic Snake & Ladder game where technical questions replace the dice.
    4. How It Works:
      1. Risk Choice: Teams can choose Easy (1–3 steps) or Hard (4–6 steps) questions.
      2. Answer Time: 30 seconds per question.
      3. Outcome: Correct → Move forward; Wrong → Stay in place.
      4. Board Dynamics: Landing on a Ladder allows climbing up, while landing on a Snake results in sliding down.
      5. Win Condition: The first team to reach Square 50 wins.
